About TrainMate

TrainMate helps travelers across India connect with fellow passengers on the same train journey. Whether you're looking for a conversation partner, someone to share meals with, or just want to make new friends during your trip, TrainMate makes it easy to find and connect with peers.

Simply register your journey or search for others traveling on the same route, and start connecting with your travel companions before you even board the train!
